Tags

Tags are versatile accessories that serve multiple purposes in fashion. They provide essential product information, enhance brand identity, and add a stylish touch, making each piece unique and recognizable.

Strings

Garment strings are essential accessories used for securing tags and labels on clothing items. They enhance presentation and organization, ensuring that important information is easily accessible while adding a polished look to garments.

Buttons

Garment buttons are essential accessories that fasten clothing securely while adding style. Available in various designs and materials, they enhance the look of outfits and contribute to overall garment appeal.

About Us

SHAH TEX LABEL LTD. is 100% Compliance Labels and accessories manufacturing company established in 2000. With in a decade SHAH TEX has expanded from local market to international well recognized market. Here, more than 300 employees are working with compliance facilities.

SHAH TEX has it's own 60,000 Sft production floor (Total two units). Also SHAH TEX belongs the latest international branded machineries. We are devoted to our quality and commitment for the better service of our valuable clients.
